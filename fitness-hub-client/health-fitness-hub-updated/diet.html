Choose Your Plan

Weight Gain (Clean Bulk)

  • Target: +250 to +350 kcal/day above TDEE.
  • Protein: 1.6–2.2 g/kg; distribute over 3–5 meals.
  • Prioritize carb sources around training (rice, potatoes, roti, fruit).
  • Healthy fats: nuts, avocado, eggs, fish, olive/mustard oil.
MealExample
BreakfastMilk oats + banana + peanut butter
LunchSteamed rice + chicken/fish + vegetables
SnackYogurt + fruit + mixed nuts
DinnerRoti + egg curry/paneer + salad

Weight Loss (Cut)

  • Target: –400 to –600 kcal/day below TDEE.
  • Protein: 1.6–2.2 g/kg to keep muscle.
  • High-volume foods: lentils, veggies, yogurt, lean meats, fruit.
  • Plan snacks: fruit, boiled eggs, chana, air-popped popcorn.
MealExample
BreakfastGreek yogurt + oats + berries
LunchRice/khichuri + grilled fish + mixed veggies
SnackLentil soup / chana chaat
DinnerRoti + chicken & salad (light oil)

Smart Habits

Hydration

  • 2–3 L/day baseline; more if hot or training.
  • Include watery foods: cucumber, oranges, soups.
  • Spread intake; check urine is pale straw color.

Meal Prep

  • Cook basics (rice, lentils, chicken) in batches 2–3×/week.
  • Keep quick add-ons: eggs, frozen veggies, yogurt, fruit, nuts.
  • Plan 1–2 “fallback” meals for busy days.

Consistency

  • Hit protein daily; allow 10–15% calorie wiggle room.
  • Lift 2–4×/week + 5–8k steps/day for best results.
  • Sleep 7–9 h; manage stress (see Mind).
